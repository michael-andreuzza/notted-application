## Description

Notted is the fastest way to jot down lists and notes. No account needed, no cloud sync, no complexity — just open and start writing.

• Open the app and start typing immediately
• Start with built-in templates or create your own
• Checked items automatically sink to the bottom
• Shake your phone to clear completed items
• Save any note as a reusable template
• Beautiful light and dark themes
• Available in English, Spanish, German & Swedish
• Works completely offline

Perfect for grocery lists, daily to-dos, quick reminders, meeting notes, and packing lists.

Your notes stay on your device. No account required, no data collection, no tracking.

Pay once, own forever. No subscriptions, no ads.
