## Full Description

Notted is the fastest note taking app for quick lists and simple notes. No account needed, no cloud sync, no complexity — just open and start writing.

• Open the app and start typing immediately
• Start with built-in templates or create your own
• Checked items automatically move to the bottom
• Shake your phone to clear completed tasks
• Save any note as a reusable template
• Light and dark theme support
• Available in English, Spanish, German & Swedish
• Works completely offline

Perfect for grocery lists, daily to-dos, meeting notes, packing lists, and quick tasks.

Your notes stay on your device. No account required, no data collection, no tracking.

Pay once, own forever. No subscriptions, no ads.
